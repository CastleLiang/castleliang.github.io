Yuxuan Liang

Assistant Professor, INTR & DSA Thrust, HKUST(GZ)
Leading the CityMind Lab

Dr. Yuxuan Liang is an Assistant Professor at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). Prior to that, he obtained his PhD degree from the School of Computing at National University of Singapore. He has published 120+ papers in leading journals and conferences, including TPAMI, AI Journal, TKDE, TMC, IJGIS, KDD, NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, and WWW. His publications have received 13,000+ citations on Google Scholar, with an h-index of 56. Based on his contributions to spatio-temporal data mining and urban computing, he was recognized as Stanford/Elsevier Top 2% Scientists, and has received multiple awards both domestically and internationally.

Our team centers on the innovation of machine learning and data mining for spatio-temporal data and time series data, connecting methodological advances with high-impact applications, such as transportation, climate, energy, and environment.
